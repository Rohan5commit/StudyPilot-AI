# Elevator Pitch

StudySprint AI is a student study copilot that turns raw notes into a structured revision workflow in minutes. Instead of forcing students to manually rewrite notes, build flashcards, and guess what to study next, the app generates a concise summary, revision checklist, quiz, weak-topic drills, and a study sprint plan tied to the exam date. It works instantly in demo mode and upgrades to NVIDIA NIM-powered AI generation when the key is available.
